Veteran Unemployment Rates in New York State

The following graph shows the unemployment rates for veterans in New York State over the past decade. The data indicates a fluctuating trend, with certain years showing significant increases in unemployment rates. This information is crucial for understanding the economic challenges faced by veterans and can help inform policy decisions aimed at improving their employment prospects.

About This Project

This project was created as part of my Google Data Analyst coursework and portfolio development. It focuses on visualizing veteran-related economic hardship indicators across New York State using public data.

Tools and support used in this project include:

  • U.S. Census Bureau — ACS 5-year estimates (data source)
  • RStudio — data cleaning, reshaping, and export to analysis-ready files
  • Tableau Public — interactive charts and dashboards embedded throughout this site
  • ChatGPT — assistance with code troubleshooting, formatting, and page structure
  • AITubo AI — image generation used for page illustrations

Notes: ACS 5-year estimates are survey-based and can be sensitive to small denominators in small geographies. Downloads are provided for transparency and reuse.
