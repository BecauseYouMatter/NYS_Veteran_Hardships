About This Project

This project was created as part of my Google Data Analyst coursework and portfolio development. It uses publicly available U.S. Census Bureau ACS 5 year estimates to visualize veteran related hardship indicators across New York State at the ZIP code level, with a focus on changes between 2019 and 2024. The goal is to support clearer, place based conversations about where veterans may need additional support and where community conditions affect veterans and nonveterans alike.

I focused on this topic because I have spent much of my career working directly with veterans on employment and transition barriers, including delivering transition assistance instruction and workforce reintegration training. Recently, I moved into an employer outreach role focused on building bridges between employers and qualified veterans. My background also includes public service, higher education instruction and support, and prior work in a range of skilled trades and technical roles. This combination of veteran services and hands on technical experience is what led me to build a data driven, community level view of where hardships appear to be concentrated.

Perspective and Transparency: I am a United States Marine Corps disabled combat veteran. While this project is grounded in publicly available ACS 5 year estimates, my lived experience and career in veteran services shape my interest in this topic and influence the questions I choose to prioritize. This is not a peer reviewed academic study or a triple blind review; rather, it is an analysis intended to present trends for discussion and to highlight actionable insights that regional averages may overlook.

Learn more about my work and background on LinkedIn: www.linkedin.com/in/mrmatthewflynn

Tools and support used in this project include:

  • U.S. Census Bureau — ACS 5-year estimates (data source)
  • RStudio — data cleaning, reshaping, and export to analysis-ready files
  • Tableau Public — interactive charts and dashboards embedded throughout this site
  • ChatGPT — assistance with code troubleshooting, formatting, and page structure
  • Gemini AI — Assistance with narrative editing, structural logic, and ensuring data alignment with institutional veteran support standards
  • AITubo AI — image generation used for page illustrations

Notes: ACS 5-year estimates are survey-based and can be sensitive to small denominators in small geographies. Downloads are provided for transparency and reuse.
